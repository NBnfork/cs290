Callista and Amy's partnership began in 2011. Since then their collaborative poetry has appeared in various journals, including Heron Tree, Spiral Orb, Stone Highway Review, and BOAAT, and was recently featured in The Inflectionist Review. They regularly lead workshops on collaborative writing, including at the Creative Writing Studies Conference, Steel Pen, and Winter Wheat.
